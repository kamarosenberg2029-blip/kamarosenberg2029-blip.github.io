Pancakes

Here's a recipe for yummy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3 tablespoons oil

    • Directions

    1. take a cup of flour and put it in a medium sized bowl.
    2. add baking powder in to the bowl and mix.
    3. add milk and egg to the dry ingredients.
    4. take the sugar and add it in before the oil.
    5. mix everything together.
    6. put some oil in a medium sized pan and cook pancakes until they start to bubble on top and flip it.
