Salmon

Here's a recipe for baked salmon.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs salmon
  • brown mustard
  • maple syrup

Directions

  1. take a cup and cover the bottom with brown mustard.
  2. cover mustard with maple syrup and mix together .
  3. place tin foil in pan and place salmon over it.
  4. cover salmon in sauce
  5. cover salmon with tin foil.
  6. put pan in the oven for 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.
